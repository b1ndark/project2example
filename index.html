History Quiz Instructions!

  1. First press Start
  2. Once the difficulty menu shows you will be able to select the difficulty mode
  3. After selecting the difficulty mode, you will be taken to the quiz
  4. Where you will have four answer options for each question
  5. Once the Answer is selected it will take you to the next question in 2 seconds
  6. Each mode has 10 questions
  7. The goal will be to get the highest score possible so you can be on the top of the score board!
  8. Have fun and enjoy the quiz :)
